## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Audit

### What is GEO?
Generative Engine Optimization is about ensuring Duininck shows up in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Claude, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, etc.). As more people ask AI "who is the best [X] contractor in [Y]?", being in the training data and having structured, authoritative content matters enormously.

### GEO Strategy Recommendations

#### What Gets You Into AI Results
1. **Wikipedia presence** — Most AI models heavily weight Wikipedia. Duininck may qualify for a page (100 years, Minnesota Family Business Award, significant company)
2. **Wikidata entry** — Structured data that AI crawls
3. **Industry directories** — Listed with correct, consistent information
4. **Press/news coverage** — Trade publications, local news, award announcements
5. **Schema markup** — Organization schema, FAQ schema, project schema on website
6. **Authoritative content** — Detailed project pages, case studies, company history
7. **Reviews and ratings** — Google Business reviews, industry ratings
8. **LinkedIn company page** — Complete, active, well-described
9. **Crunchbase** — Company profile (even for non-tech companies)

#### GEO Content Opportunities
- [ ] Comprehensive "About" page with full history (AI loves structured narratives)
- [ ] Detailed project pages with specifications (not just photos)
- [ ] FAQ pages answering common industry questions
- [ ] Blog/insights covering industry topics with authoritative voice
- [ ] Team/leadership bios with credentials
- [ ] Awards and recognition page (centralized)
- [ ] Service area pages for each location

#### Nicole's Goal Alignment
Nicole is already using AI tools (Claude, Co-pilot, ChatGPT). She understands the shift. GEO strategy aligns with her digital-first mindset and gives Duininck a competitive edge that slow-moving construction competitors won't pursue for years.
