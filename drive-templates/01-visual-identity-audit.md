## Photography Style

### Based on Nicole Discovery (CONFIRMED)
| Principle | Details |
|---|---|
| **No talking heads** | Blue-collar workforce won't go on camera. Don't force it. |
| **Film the work** | Equipment, job sites, process, progress. The work IS the content. |
| **Cinematic b-roll** | Think documentary style, not corporate video. |
| **Drone footage** | Aerial shots of job sites, courses, communities. Scale and impact. |
| **Voiceover narratives** | Story told by narrator or over work footage, not by on-camera talent. |
| **Timing offset** | Golf beauty shots come a YEAR after construction. Plan captures now. |

### Shot Categories
1. **Active work** — Equipment, crews, materials, paving, grading
2. **Aerial/drone** — Job site scope, course layouts, community context
3. **Before/during/after** — Project transformation sequences (carousels)
4. **Equipment** — Trucks, machinery, fleet (the "full truck" visual)
5. **Culture** — Team moments, safety, training (candid, not posed)
6. **Completed projects** — Final results, community impact
7. **Seasonal** — Four-season documentation of the same projects
