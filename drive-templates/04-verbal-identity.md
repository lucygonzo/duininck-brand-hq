## Brand Voice

### Voice Summary
The Duininck voice is a **firm handshake**: warm, genuine, confident, and rooted. Not corporate polish. Not folksy simplicity. The voice of people who build real things and keep their word.

### Voice Traits (Validated by CMO Discovery Call)
| Trait | Description | Sounds Like | Doesn't Sound Like |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Steadfast** | Reliable, consistent, proven over generations. 100 years proves it. | "We've been doing this longer than most companies have existed." | "We're disrupting the construction industry." |
| **Humble** | Let the work speak. The dirt under the fingernails is real. | "Here's what we built this year." (shows photos) | "We're the best construction company in the Midwest." |
| **Generous** | With time, talent, and resources. Giving is a reflex, not a strategy. | "Our people give back because that's who we are." | "Our CSR program demonstrates our commitment to ESG." |
| **Forward-Looking** | Rooted in tradition but building for the next generation. | "100 years taught us that the best is still ahead." | "We're a legacy company." (sounds like past tense) |
| **Grounded** | Connected to the land, the work, the people, the values. | "We build roads. We build courses. We build people." | "Our innovative solutions leverage synergistic capabilities." |

## Voice Spectrum

| Axis | Position | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Formal ← → Casual | Center-formal | Professional but approachable. Never stiff. |
| Bold ← → Understated | Understated | Let the work speak. Confidence without bravado. |
| Technical ← → Plain | Plain with authority | Use industry language only when it adds clarity. |
| Serious ← → Playful | Serious-warm | Not stiff, not joking. Warm and substantial. |

## Language Rules

### We Say ✅
- Built to last — 100 years and counting
- People. Values. Growth.
- Four generations strong
- When our truck pulls up, you get all of us
- We see the whole person
- Building for the next generation
- The foundation matters
- Enduring partnerships

### We Never Say ❌
- Disrupting the industry
- Move fast and break things
- Crushing it
- Innovative solutions (too startup-y)
- Synergistic alignment
- Best-in-class (overused, unsubstantiated)
- We're like a family (they ARE a family — don't dilute it with a cliche)
- Leveraging our capabilities
- End-to-end solutions
