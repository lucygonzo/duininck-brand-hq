## Heritage Claim: VALIDATED

Ulland Brothers was founded in 1920, making them **106 years old and 6 years older than Duininck** (1926). They celebrated their centennial around 2020 (confirmed by Duluth News Tribune coverage). Their founding story: Oscar and Palmer Ulland started with "a hand saw, an old Ford truck and a simple philosophy."

This is significant. Our competitive landscape previously stated that no competitor could match Duininck's 100-year heritage claim. **That is incorrect.** Ulland Brothers can match it and exceed it by 6 years.

### How This Changes the Wedge

The 100-year claim is no longer unique in MN heavy civil. However, the wedge still holds because:

1. **Generational depth**: Ulland's website does NOT mention which generation currently runs the company. No family members are named on the current site. Duininck has 4 documented generations with Gen 4 (Sam) actively in the business. The generational story may be deeper.
2. **Portfolio breadth**: Ulland does excavating and paving. Duininck does construction + golf courses + water management + real estate. "The Full Truck" still carries more.
3. **Geographic overlap is limited**: Ulland operates from Cloquet (NE MN), Virginia (Iron Range), and Albert Lea (southern MN). Duininck is based in Willmar/Prinsburg (central MN). They may rarely compete head-to-head on the same projects.
4. **Values depth**: Ulland's philosophy is "keep your word, do your best work." Functional but not distinctive. Duininck's 6-dimension wellbeing framework and faith-based values are more differentiated.
