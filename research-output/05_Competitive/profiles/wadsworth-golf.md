## Vulnerabilities

1. **No parent company.** Wadsworth is a standalone golf construction company. They don't have their own aggregates, heavy equipment fleet from a construction division, or infrastructure expertise. Duininck Golf is backed by a $100M+ family company. That's cost advantages, equipment access, and operational depth.
2. **67 years vs. 100 years.** When the parent company story is told, Duininck's heritage is deeper.
3. **"Premier" is self-declared.** No third party gave them this title. It's marketing. Duininck could counter with specific prestige claims (named architect restorations).
4. **Charitable foundation could be matched.** $6M in grants is impressive, but Duininck's values-driven generosity is arguably more embedded in the culture than a separate foundation.

---

## Specific Threats to Duininck Golf

1. **The "1,000+ projects" claim** is the biggest volume differentiator. Duininck Golf needs to quantify their project count.
2. **"44 states"** signals national reach that Duininck Golf (MN + GA base) may not match publicly.
3. **NGF Top 100** recognition is a third-party validation Duininck Golf should pursue.
4. **The charitable foundation** gives Wadsworth a "gives back" narrative that resonates with golf communities.
