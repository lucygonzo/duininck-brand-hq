## SEO Baseline

### Search Visibility by Query

| Search Query | Duininck Position | Who Ranks Instead | Opportunity |
|-------------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| "heavy civil contractor Minnesota" | NOT in top 10 | Park Construction, Valley Paving, Central Specialties, KGM | HIGH |
| "road construction company Willmar MN" | Likely top 3 (branded + local) | Local competitors | OWNED |
| "paving contractor Minnesota" | NOT in top 10 | Valley Paving, DMJ Asphalt, others | HIGH |
| "golf course builder" | NOT in top 10 | Wadsworth Golf, MacCurrach, Turco, Aspen | CRITICAL |
| "golf course renovation company" | NOT in top 10 | Wadsworth, MacCurrach, DHR Construction | CRITICAL |
| "golf course construction" | NOT in top 10 | Wadsworth Golf dominates | CRITICAL |
| "drainage pipe manufacturer" | NOT in top 10 (for Prinsco) | ADS and others likely dominate | HIGH |
| "Duininck Companies" | #1 (branded search) | n/a | OWNED |
| "Duininck" | #1 (branded search) | n/a | OWNED |

### Observations
- **Duininck is invisible for non-branded searches.** If someone doesn't already know the name, they won't find Duininck through Google.
- **Branded searches work fine.** "Duininck" and "Duininck Companies" return the correct sites.
- **Competitive keywords are owned by competitors**: Valley Paving, Park Construction, and Central Specialties rank for MN construction terms. Wadsworth Golf dominates golf construction terms.
- **Local SEO may be the biggest quick win.** Duininck operates in specific geographies where localized search terms ("construction company Willmar MN") would be less competitive.

### Critical SEO Issues Found

1. **Outdated meta description on duininck.com**: Says "third generation" when they're in 4th gen. Google displays this in search results. Every searcher sees incorrect information.
2. **Prinsco meta description is "Water management solutions"**: This is 3 words. Meta descriptions should be 150-160 characters with keywords, value proposition, and CTA.
3. **No individual project pages**: Without unique URLs for individual projects, there's nothing for Google to index for project-specific or location-specific searches.
4. **No blog/content strategy on duininckgolf.com**: Zero content for Google to index beyond 6 static pages.
5. **Duininck Concrete still appears in search results**: The brand is being retired but pages may still be live, creating confusion.
6. **Multiple domains splitting authority**: SEO value is fragmented across duininckcompanies.com, duininck.com, duininckgolf.com, and prinsco.com. Backlinks and authority don't compound.

## Visibility Strategy Findings (Discovery, No Recommendations)

### What's Working
1. **Branded search is clean.** Searching "Duininck" returns the correct properties.
2. **Prinsco has a content hub.** "The Water Table" is an educational resource for ag water management. This is the most sophisticated content play in the portfolio.
3. **duininck.com has the deepest site structure.** Capabilities, markets, products, leadership, testimonials. It's the most complete website in the portfolio.
4. **Duininck Golf is GCBAA certified.** This credential appears on the About page.
5. **Instagram has 416 posts** on @duininckinc. There IS content being produced. The audience just isn't finding it.

### What's Not Working
1. **No non-branded search visibility.** Outside of branded queries, Duininck doesn't rank for any competitive keyword observed.
2. **6 websites, 6 separate SEO silos.** Domain authority doesn't transfer between properties. A backlink to prinsco.com does nothing for duininckcompanies.com.
3. **No project case study pages.** Without individual project URLs, there's no content for Google to index for project-specific or location-specific searches. Competitors like Ames have project pages that rank for specific terms.
4. **Glassdoor presence is threadbare.** 9 reviews vs. Ames (114) and Knife River (141). This affects recruiting visibility and employer brand perception.
5. **Golf division social is dark.** The most visually compelling content opportunity (beautiful courses, drone footage, prestige venues) has zero active social distribution.
6. **Meta descriptions are wrong or empty.** The most basic SEO element is neglected. duininck.com says "third generation" (wrong). prinsco.com says "Water management solutions" (wasted).
7. **No Google Business Profiles verified.** BBB listings exist but GBP status is unknown. For local searches in Willmar, Prinsburg, Rapid City, this is critical.
8. **No Wikipedia or Wikidata presence.** This matters for GEO (AI discoverability). AI models cite Wikipedia frequently.
9. **No industry directory optimization.** GCBAA certification exists for Golf, but AGC/NAPA/ENR directory presence is unknown. These directories feed both SEO and GEO.
10. **Centennial is invisible online.** The biggest brand moment in 100 years has zero digital presence. No landing page, no countdown, no content, no press page.

### Competitor Digital Practices Observed
- **Ames Construction**: 35,000 LinkedIn followers. They've invested in employer brand content and industry thought leadership. Blocked our web scrape (they protect their content).
- **Knife River**: Instagram handle is @lifeatknife (employer brand focused). 22,000 Facebook likes. They've built social around the employee experience, not just projects.
- **Valley Paving**: Ranks for "Minnesota road construction contractor" with a clean, focused website. They OWN their geographic niche digitally.
- **Wadsworth Golf**: Ranks for "golf course construction" terms. Their website positions them as "America's Premier Golf Course Builder" (exact claim).
- **Central Specialties**: "Third-generation, award-winning, family-owned business." They use the same family heritage play but with 40 years (vs. Duininck's 100). And they rank higher.

### Additional Competitors Discovered During Audit
The search audit revealed MN-market competitors not previously on our list:
- **Ulland Brothers**: Over 100 years in Minnesota (similar heritage claim to Duininck)
- **Park Construction**: Heavy earth moving, structural work, demolition, paving
- **Valley Paving**: Road construction since 1978, strong local SEO
- **Central Specialties**: 3rd gen family-owned, 40+ years, award-winning
- **KGM Contractors**: 50+ years, Northeast MN highway heavy contractor
- **Hoover Construction**: Family-owned highway heavy, 50+ years, NE Minnesota

Of note: **Ulland Brothers claims 100+ years in Minnesota.** They may be the only heritage-comparable competitor. Research needed: is their claim verified? Are they competing for the same projects?

## Google Business Profile Status

### What We Found
- BBB profiles exist for Duininck Incorporated (Prinsburg) and Duininck Concrete (Alexandria, Willmar)
- BBB profiles are NOT accredited
- Google Business Profile verification status could not be confirmed from search alone
- No Google reviews surfaced in search results

### What This Means
If GBPs are not claimed and optimized, Duininck is invisible in Google Maps and Local Pack results. When a county engineer searches "construction company near Willmar MN," Duininck should appear in the map pack with photos, reviews, hours, and contact info. Without a GBP, competitors with claimed profiles get that visibility.
