# Persona: Current Employees (700+ Field Workers)
> The most undertapped brand ambassadors in the company
> Priority: CRITICAL (Internal)
> Last Updated: March 2026
> Status: Research Complete

---

## The Communication Challenge
- 700+ employees, ~90% in the field across 5+ states
- No corporate email for most field workers
- No intranet or internal comms platform
- Communication happens through managers and job site huddles
- Industry data: 83% of frontline workers globally don't have a corporate email address
- 45% don't have access to company intranet at work
- Email used by 90% of head office leaders vs. only 27% for frontline managers

Nicole: "We have over 700 employees that are out in the field that aren't getting the information. This company is just amazing... the story is so rich."

---

## How Other Companies Solve This

| Approach | Examples | Pros | Cons |
|----------|----------|------|------|
| Mobile app (employee comms) | Beekeeper, Staffbase, LumApps | Push notifications. Photo/video sharing. Surveys. | Requires app download. Adoption can lag. |
| SMS-based | Team Engine, Heymarket | Reaches everyone. No app needed. | Limited to text. Not visual. |
| WhatsApp groups | Common in smaller companies | Free. Already on phones. | Hard to manage at 700+ scale. |
| Digital signage at break areas | Common in manufacturing | Passive reach. | Only works at fixed locations. |
| Printed materials (toolbox talks) | Traditional approach | Familiar format. | One-way, no engagement tracking. |

---

## What Content Would Reach This Audience
- Centennial pride: "You're part of something 100 years in the making"
- Safety recognition: "Crew X completed 365 days incident-free"
- Project spotlights: "Here's what YOUR work looks like from a drone"
- Benefits reminders: Wellness programs, tuition reimbursement, 401k match
- Seasonal kickoff/wrap: Mark the start and end of construction season
- Family events: Content their spouses and kids see too

---

## Key Finding
If each of 700+ employees shared one piece of content per year with their personal network, that's 700+ authentic impressions in the communities where Duininck recruits. The internal comms strategy isn't about informing employees. It's about turning them into a volunteer marketing army, but only if they feel genuinely connected to the story.
