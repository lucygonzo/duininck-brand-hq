# Verbal Identity Inventory: Duininck Companies
> Every tagline, headline, value statement, and messaging fragment currently live across all properties
> Last Updated: March 2026
> Status: Scraped and documented

---

## Why This Matters
Before designing new voice guidelines or messaging, we need to see exactly what language exists today. This inventory reveals: inconsistencies between properties, outdated claims (like "third generation"), messaging gaps, and the raw material for unified voice guidelines.

---

## Critical Finding: Three Different Companies, Three Different Voices

The three main Duininck websites sound like three different companies:

| Property | Voice | Tone | Focus |
|----------|-------|------|-------|
| duininckcompanies.com | Corporate, portfolio-level | Warm, family-focused | Values, stories, people |
| duininck.com | Operational, contractor-speak | Practical, capability-focused | Services, markets, projects |
| duininckgolf.com | Premium, craft-oriented | Confident, partnership-focused | Quality, expertise, peace of mind |

This isn't inherently wrong (different audiences need different tones), but there's currently no connective tissue. Nothing on duininck.com references the family story. Nothing on duininckgolf.com mentions the 100-year heritage. The portfolio site talks about values; the construction site talks about capabilities. They need a bridge.

## VALUES CONFLICT: Two Different Value Sets

**duininckcompanies.com lists:**
1. Stewardship
2. Integrity
3. Servant Leadership
4. Family
5. Generosity

**duininck.com/about/values/ lists:**
1. Safety First
2. Team Duininck
3. Value Of Each Person
4. Mind The Gap
5. Get It Done

These are fundamentally different value systems. The parent site has legacy/faith-based values. The construction site has operational/cultural values. Both are valid, but which is the REAL set? This needs resolution in the brand unification. Nicole should clarify whether these are meant to coexist (corporate values vs. operating values) or whether one replaces the other.
